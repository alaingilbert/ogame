Let’s take a look at lifeforms. The larger a civilisation, the more developed the technologies it has at its disposal. The first thing you need to expand are the Biosphere Farm and Residential Sector to ensure our population has sufficient food and comfortable living quarters. Once our population has expanded enough, we’ll gain access to the technology tree.

  • Upgrade the Biosphere Farm lifeform building to level 22
  • Upgrade the Residential Sector lifeform building to level 21

Next we need a Research Centre. Lifeform technologies are powerful as they apply across your entire empire. That means that all the research on this planet will apply to every planet in your empire. Our researchers promise me that they really do need somewhere new to place all the necessary equipment, and they’re not just trying to blag some shiny new offices. So go and build a level 1 Research Centre!
